About the Trust

Wildwood is proud to be a member of BIAZA (British and Irish Association of Zoos and Aquariums).

BIAZA represents the best zoos and aquariums in Britain and Ireland and helps to promote their work. As a member of BIAZA, Wildwood takes pride maintaining the highest standards of animal welfare, education and conservation work.
